Photos.

These are some photos taken in the last few years, They are in no particular order, but the oldest were taken with a Fuji X-E1, then mainly with an X-T1, and the newest with an X-T3. Lenses are Fuji 16mm f2.8, 35mm f1.4, 55-200mm f3.5-4.8, Samyang 12mm f2, Viltrox 85mm f1.8.

Locations here include the Lake District, Florence and Siena, Lincoln, and the Peak District.
